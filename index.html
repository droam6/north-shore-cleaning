What We Offer

Sydney's Trusted Cleaning Experts

At North Shore Cleaning, we deliver spotless results that make your home or workplace shine. Whether you need a regular house clean, end of lease clean, or a thorough spring clean, our experienced team ensures every surface is left sparkling.

We use eco-friendly, high-quality cleaning products that are safe for your family and pets while being tough on dirt and grime. Every clean comes with our satisfaction guarantee.

  • House Cleaning Regular and one-off house cleans covering all rooms, bathrooms, kitchens, and living areas to a sparkling standard.
  • End of Lease Cleaning Comprehensive bond cleans that meet real estate inspection standards. Bond-back guarantee included.
  • Office Cleaning Professional commercial cleaning for offices, retail spaces, and strata common areas with flexible scheduling.
  • Carpet Cleaning Deep carpet steam cleaning and stain removal that restores your carpets to like-new condition.
  • Window Cleaning Streak-free interior and exterior window cleaning for homes and commercial properties.
  • Spring Cleaning Deep cleaning service covering every corner of your home — inside cupboards, behind appliances, and more.
